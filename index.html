AI for Good: HealthNeem

HealthNeem applies AI to a high-impact public problem: making health information more understandable, accessible, and culturally usable for regional-language communities.

HealthNeem is positioned as an AI-powered health-literacy platform for regional-language access, using trusted-source curation, bilingual terminology handling, and user-centered health education.

  • AI-powered health-literacy platform for Telugu and regional-language users.
  • Reported hundreds of thousands of views within months of launch.
  • Reported over one million events in early media coverage.
  • Recognized with multiple MarCom Gold Awards, a Davey Silver Award, and HDM Awards Silver recognition.
  • Covered by Entrepreneur, New Indian Express, Eenadu, Andhra Jyothy, and EdexLive.
